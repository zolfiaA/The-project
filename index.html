Think Global

About us

Think Global is an NGO that offers international exchange programs. The team at Think Global believe that the only way to truly experience a society is to immerse 100% into a culture and society of your choice as a member of their adopted family. We believe that by having a candid and unfiltered experience fully immersed will travellers develop appreciation and respect for the diversity of the world.

What we have to offer

There are numerous types of ‘lifestyles’ spanning 190 countries.Applicants can immerse in rural life in their chosen country.With options ranging from living in a yurt with a traditional family in the vast plains of rural Mongolia to living with a berber family in North Africa and more.

Although Think Global offers various types of international exchanges the company focuses on the authentic immersion in the host culture.With the trend of international exchange,Think Global has specialized in the business of authentic, candid back to basics immersion.
